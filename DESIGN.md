# Design System Document

This document outlines the core aesthetic and functional principles of our design system, as reflected in our current theme.

## 1. Color Palette

Our color palette is designed for impact and clarity, operating in a **dark** mode environment.

*   **Primary Color**: `#B22222` - This bold red serves as our primary brand identifier, used for key interactive elements and calls to action.
*   **Secondary Color**: `#32CD32` - A vibrant lime green, providing contrast and support for secondary actions and information.
*   **Tertiary Color**: `#FFFFFF` - Pure white, used as an accent for highlights and decorative elements against the darker palette.
*   **Neutral Color**: `#0D0D0D` - A very dark grey, forming the foundation of our backgrounds and non-chromatic surfaces.

## 2. Typography

Our typography system combines modern and legible fonts to ensure readability and hierarchy.

*   **Headline Font**: `epilogue` - Used for all major headings and titles, conveying a strong and contemporary presence.
*   **Body Font**: `spaceGrotesk` - Selected for its excellent readability in paragraph text and longer content blocks.
*   **Label Font**: `spaceGrotesk` - Applied to labels, buttons, and smaller text elements for consistency and clarity.

## 3. Shape and Form

The visual treatment of our UI elements emphasizes a modern aesthetic with subtle softness.

*   **Roundedness**: `1` (Subtle roundedness) - Corners of UI elements have a slight curve, providing a contemporary and approachable feel without being overly soft or sharp.

## 4. Spacing

Our spacing guidelines ensure a balanced and organized layout, promoting readability and ease of interaction.

*   **Spacing**: `2` (Normal) - We maintain a normal level of whitespace, providing adequate separation between elements for a clean and efficient interface.
